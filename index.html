School Life

Rooftop Turf

The Rooftop is normally where we have all our sports events. It is a lot of fun to play Football on the turf. We also have a 50 meter pool. The rooftop is my favourite place in the school because it is a lot of fun to play Football over there.

Auditorium

I really like the Auditorium because it is very big and useful. We use the Auditorium for plays,musical's, movie's or an important announcement. When there is an important announce mostly everyone for Grade 1-5 come to the Auditorium. It is also very entertaining to watch what is being shown.

Lobby

I like the lobby because thats the first place I see when I enter the building. It is also where you meet up with your friends before you go up for your classes. The lobby is a very nice place also because you can just chat and do nothing till you go up.
